Welcome

We are a research lab at IIT Mandi focused on powder metallurgy and advanced materials processing. Our work bridges fundamental understanding and practical applications in structural and functional materials.

Who We Are

The Powder Metallurgical Processing Laboratory (PMPL) is led by Dr. V.M. Suntharavel Muthaiah. The lab explores materials design, processing, and property optimization for structural integrity and performance under extreme conditions. Our interdisciplinary approach combines materials science, mechanical engineering, and nanotechnology.

Research Projects

  • High Entropy Alloys (HEAs) for structural applications
  • Accident-tolerant fuel cladding materials
  • Nanoindentation and deformation mechanics
  • Oxidation behavior of coated and uncoated alloys
  • Diffusion barrier development using pack cementation
